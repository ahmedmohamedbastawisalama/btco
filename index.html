BTCO


A New Standard in Creative Trade


BTCO is Bahrain's first international B2B tradeshow dedicated to excellence in fine jewelry, fashion, design, and the arts.


Our mission is to establish Bahrain as a vibrant creative and commercial hub—bridging local talent with global markets.


Rooted in Bahrain's rich heritage and inspired by its dynamic future, BTCO offers a curated experience that seamlessly blends authenticity, innovation, and elegance.

What We Offer



- Premium B2B Tradeshow with 180 curated exhibitors


- VIP Cradle Tours across Bahrain's cultural heritage sites


- Digital platform with e-commerce and storytelling features


- Conferences, panels, and workshops led by global experts


- Strategic matchmaking with global retailers and embassies


Our Purpose



Empowering Creative Excellence


At BTCO, we believe in the transformative power of creativity. Our platform champions inclusive economic growth by promoting women, youth, and cultural industries— aligning with Bahrain Economic Vision 2030.


We showcase local craftsmanship on a global stage, drive trade, innovation, and strategic partnerships, and build lasting visibility for Bahrain's creative economy.
